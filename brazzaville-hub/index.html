Welcome to Brazzaville Tech Learners Hub

A community space for students and professionals learning software development and AI — meetups, tutorials, and practical API integrations.

Our mission

We connect local learners with resources, hands-on practice, and community events to accelerate skills in web development, data, and AI. The Hub emphasizes accessibility, open learning, and real-world project experience.

Opportunities

  • Weekly study groups and coding dojos
  • Guest talks and workshops with industry mentors
  • Project showcases and portfolio-building assistance
  • Access to curated learning resources and APIs

Get involved

Attend an event, contribute resources, or propose a workshop. Visit the Events page to see upcoming meetups, or the Resources page for learning materials.
